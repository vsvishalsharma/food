Get ready for a delicious ride

  • Heat the spices in oil: Heat the oil in a small pan over medium heat, then cook the coriander, cumin, turmeric, cayenne, garam masala and paprika, stirring often, until fragrant (approximately 2-3 minutes). Let cool completely.


  • Whisk the cooled spice-oil mixture into the yogurt, then mix in the lemon juice, garlic, salt and ginger.


  • Cut deep slashes into chicken, coat with marinade, chill


  • Prepare your grill so that one side is quite hot over direct heat, the other side cooler, not over direct heat


  • Shake off excess marinade, place chicken on hot side of grill




  • Move chicken to the cool side of the grill. Cover and cook for at least 20 minutes
